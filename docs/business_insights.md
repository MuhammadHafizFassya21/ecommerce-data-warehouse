## 8. Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can be made:

1. Prioritize high-performing product categories for marketing and stock planning.
2. Monitor sellers with high late delivery rates, especially sellers with high transaction volume.
3. Improve delivery operations in customer states with high revenue and high late delivery rates.
4. Maintain payment methods that contribute the highest transaction value.
5. Use monthly revenue trends to plan promotional campaigns and inventory readiness.
